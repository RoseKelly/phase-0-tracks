Robot using an elevator:

-Start at the bottom row of buttons on the left-most side of the button columns
-Count beginning with this button and call it #1
  -Move right, stopping at each button, and add 1 each time until you run out of buttons on that row
  -Move your finger up one row of buttons and all the way over to the left and continue counting in the same direction as the step above, again adding 1 each time
  -Continue this until you have reached the number you are looking for
-Press your finger forward to select the button 


Taking the train to work:

-Wait on platform until train arrives
-When doors open, enter train
-When train stops, listen for "Monroe"
  -If train stop is not "Monroe", continue on the train
  -If train stop is "Monroe", exit the train
    -Navigate toward the office
